How to Get the FBF App
4 quick steps — you'll be set up in under 2 minutes
1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and create a password. Do this first — you'll need it to log in to the app.

fbf-dashboard.vercel.app/signup →
2

Install TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official app for testing beta apps. Install it from the App Store (it's free).

Download TestFlight →
3

Install the FBF App

Tap this link on your iPhone to join the beta and install Forged by Freedom through TestFlight.

testflight.apple.com/join/Xgq6sV1t →
4

Sign In & Go

Open the app and sign in with the email and password you created in Step 1. Your coach's program will already be loaded.
