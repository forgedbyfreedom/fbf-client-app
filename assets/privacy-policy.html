Forged by Freedom LLC operates the App as a personalized fitness and nutrition coaching platform. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, phone number, and password (encrypted) provided during registration
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, height, weight, body measurements, fitness goals, medical conditions, and training preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged with your coach through our real-time chat feature

Health & Fitness Data

  • Check-In Data: Daily body metrics, nutrition intake, activity levels, sleep duration and quality, supplement compliance, mood, and notes

Photos & Media

  • Progress Photos: Images you voluntarily upload to track your physical progress

Usage & Device Data

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Push Notification Tokens: Used to deliver check-in reminders and coach messages
  • App Usage: Feature interaction data to improve the user experience

3. Apple HealthKit Data

Our App integrates with Apple HealthKit to provide a seamless fitness tracking experience. This section describes our practices specific to HealthKit data, in compliance with Apple's requirements.

HealthKit Data Commitments

  • We only read HealthKit data (steps, sleep analysis, heart rate, body temperature, and workout data). We do not write any data to Apple Health.
  • HealthKit data is not sold to data brokers, advertisers, or any other third parties.
  • HealthKit data is not shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to providing health and fitness services to you within the App.
  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or marketing purposes of any kind.
  • HealthKit data is used solely to auto-fill your daily check-in data (steps, sleep, heart rate, body temperature, and workout summaries) to reduce manual data entry and improve your coaching experience.
  • HealthKit data is not stored in iCloud by our App.
  • Access to HealthKit data requires your explicit opt-in permission via the iOS Health permissions dialog. You may revoke access at any time through your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

HealthKit data that is read by the App may be transmitted to our secure servers solely for the purpose of populating your daily check-in and enabling your assigned coach to review your fitness metrics. This data is protected by the same encryption and security standards described in Section 5 of this policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Coaching Services: To deliver personalized training programs, meal plans, supplement protocols, and coaching recommendations
  • Daily Check-Ins: To track your progress, generate trends, and provide data-driven coaching adjustments
  • AI Voice Coaching: To provide AI-powered coaching responses and guidance through our voice assistant feature
  • Communication: To enable real-time chat between you and your coach, and to send push notifications for reminders and updates
  • Gamification: To calculate streaks, award badges, and power the opt-in community leaderboard
  • Nutrition Lookup: To retrieve food nutrition data when you scan product barcodes
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Account Management: To authenticate your identity, manage your account, and provide customer support

5. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • Hosting: Your data is stored on Supabase, a secure cloud platform with SOC 2 Type II compliance, utilizing PostgreSQL databases with row-level security policies
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Account authentication is managed through Supabase Auth with secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Access Control: Your personal data and check-in history are accessible only to you, your assigned coach, and authorized administrators within your organization
  • Photo Storage: Progress photos are stored in secure cloud storage with access restricted to authenticated users

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our coaching services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your active account
  • Check-In History: Retained for the duration of your active account to enable long-term progress tracking and trend analysis
  • Chat Messages: Retained for the duration of your active account
  • Progress Photos: Retained for the duration of your active account; you may request deletion at any time
  • HealthKit Data: Temporarily processed to auto-fill check-in fields; historical HealthKit data is retained only as part of submitted check-in records

Upon account deletion or deactivation, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, legal compliance).
