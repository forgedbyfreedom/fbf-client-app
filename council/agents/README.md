## How Sessions Work

1. **Rotation**: Each session, one member leads (rotating by session index). The leader proposes a bold idea.
2. **Debate**: All 8 other members debate the proposal through their specific lens.
3. **Synthesis**: The leader revises the plan based on feedback.
4. **Vote**: All 9 members vote APPROVE / DENY / DEFER on each extracted recommendation, with reasoning.
5. **Minutes**: Official meeting minutes are generated and saved as a hard copy.
6. **Memo**: An executive summary memo is generated with action items.
7. **Delivery**: Memo, recommendations (with approve/deny/defer buttons), and split vote alerts are emailed to Bryan and Wendy.

## Special Powers

- **Victoria Sterling (Legal)** holds veto power on any recommendation that creates material legal exposure.
- **Session Leader** sets the agenda and synthesizes the final plan.
- **All members** vote independently — split votes are escalated to Bryan and Wendy for discussion.
