**Displacement Factor:** 0.943mL per gram

**Step-by-Step:**
1. Weigh 25g of Testosterone Enanthate on calibrated scale (±0.01g)
2. Add to clean glass beaker
3. Add 2mL Benzyl Alcohol (BA) — 2% concentration
4. Add 18mL Benzyl Benzoate (BB) — 18% concentration
5. Add MCT oil to reach 100mL total volume (accounting for displacement)
6. Place on magnetic stir plate, heat to 60°C for 15 minutes until fully dissolved
7. Allow to cool to ~40°C
8. Draw into 20mL syringe, attach 0.22μm PVDF filter
9. Filter into pre-sterilized vials, vent with 18G needle
10. Crimp vials with 20mm caps
11. Visual inspection: hold to light, check for particulates, clarity, color
12. Label with: compound, concentration, batch #, date, expiry
13. Crash test: refrigerate 2 vials for 48 hours — verify no crystallization

**Storage:** Room temperature, away from direct light. Shelf life: 24+ months.
**QC Notes:** Ensure solution is clear with no particulates and has a light amber color.
