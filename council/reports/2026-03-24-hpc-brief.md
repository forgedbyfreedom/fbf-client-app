- **Follistatin Gene Therapy**: Ongoing animal studies with promising muscle growth results. Human trials in early phases.

### Novel GLP-1/Multi-Agonists
- **Retatrutide**: Triple agonist (GIP/GLP-1/Glucagon), Phase 2 results show significant weight loss. Expected availability in 2028.
- **Survodutide**: Dual GCG/GLP-1 agonist, Phase 2 for obesity and NASH with positive outcomes.
- **Orforglipron**: Oral non-peptide GLP-1, could eliminate injection need. Phase 2 trials show efficacy.
- **CagriSema**: Combination of Cagrilintide and Semaglutide, promising Phase 2 results for weight management.
- **Amycretin**: Oral GLP-1/Amylin dual agonist by Novo Nordisk, in early trial phases.
