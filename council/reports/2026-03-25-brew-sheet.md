**Step-by-Step:**
1. Weigh 25g of Testosterone Enanthate on a calibrated scale (±0.01g).
2. Add to a clean glass beaker.
3. Add 2mL Benzyl Alcohol (BA) — 2% concentration.
4. Add 18mL Benzyl Benzoate (BB) — 18% concentration.

7. Allow to cool to ~40°C (must be warm enough to flow, cool enough to not damage filter).
8. Draw into a 20mL syringe, attach 0.22μm PVDF filter.
9. Filter into pre-sterilized vials, vent with 18G needle.
10. Crimp vials with 20mm caps.
11. Visual inspection: hold to light, check for particulates, clarity, color.
12. Label with: compound, concentration, batch #, date, expiry.
13. Crash test: refrigerate 2 vials for 48 hours — verify no crystallization.
